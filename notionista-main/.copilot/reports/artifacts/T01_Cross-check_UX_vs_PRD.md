# T01 Cross-check UX vs PRD Report

## Overview

This report evaluates the alignment between the UX artifacts (T01-T05) and the Product Requirements Document (PRD) for the Competitive Advantage platform. The goal is to identify matches, gaps, and actionable recommendations to ensure consistency and user-centric design.

---

## Matches

### 1. Dashboard

- **PRD Expectation:** Situational awareness in under 30 seconds with KPI tiles, configurable widgets, and quick insights.
- **UX Alignment:**
  - Clear navigation patterns for accessing KPI tiles.
  - Configurable widgets implemented with drag-and-drop functionality.
  - Quick insights prominently displayed on the dashboard.

### 2. Sales Analytics

- **PRD Expectation:** Time-series analysis, market share visualizations, and sales velocity indicators.
- **UX Alignment:**
  - Interactive charts for time-series analysis.
  - Market share visualizations integrated into the analytics dashboard.
  - Sales velocity indicators displayed as dynamic widgets.

### 3. Accessibility Standards

- **PRD Expectation:** WCAG 2.2 Level AA compliance.
- **UX Alignment:**
  - Semantic HTML and ARIA attributes implemented.
  - Visible focus indicators and keyboard navigation support.

---

## Gaps

### 1. Customer Segmentation

- **PRD Expectation:** Behavioral and demographic segmentation with churn risk scoring.
- **UX Gap:**
  - Limited visualization options for customer segmentation.
  - Churn risk scoring not prominently displayed.

### 2. Daily Operations

- **PRD Expectation:** Calendar-based sales visualization and trend-driven recommendations.
- **UX Gap:**
  - Calendar view lacks integration with trend-driven recommendations.
  - Notes per day feature is underdeveloped.

### 3. Performance Benchmarking

- **PRD Expectation:** Dispensary comparisons, industry benchmarks, and AI-generated narratives.
- **UX Gap:**
  - AI-generated narratives missing from the benchmarking section.
  - Limited options for dispensary vs. dispensary comparisons.

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Enhance Customer Segmentation

- Develop more robust visualization options for behavioral and demographic segmentation.
- Highlight churn risk scoring prominently in the customer analytics section.

### 2. Improve Daily Operations

- Integrate trend-driven recommendations into the calendar view.
- Expand the notes per day feature to include actionable insights.

### 3. Strengthen Performance Benchmarking

- Implement AI-generated narratives for performance benchmarking.
- Provide detailed dispensary comparison options with industry benchmarks.

---

## Conclusion

The UX artifacts align well with the PRD in several key areas, including the dashboard, sales analytics, and accessibility standards. However, gaps in customer segmentation, daily operations, and performance benchmarking need to be addressed to ensure full alignment with the PRD.
